About Us & Contact

Bringing joy through timeless home décor, crafted in Lesotho.

Our Story

Joy in A Box was born from a love for beautiful, functional spaces that nurture the soul. Based in Maseru, Lesotho, we collaborate with local artisans and designers to create home décor that balances modern aesthetics with natural warmth.

Every piece is thoughtfully selected or custom-made to bring a sense of calm, comfort, and joy into your home. We believe in sustainable practices, ethical craftsmanship, and designs that stand the test of time.

Our Values

  • Handcrafted excellence
  • Sustainable materials
  • Supporting local artisans
  • Timeless, minimalist design
